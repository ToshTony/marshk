Who We Are

Marshk Investments Limited is a Construction Services Provider founded with an aim of providing Telecommunication and Energy Infrastructure Services for carriers and service providers across Kenya.

With a team of experienced professionals and engineers, we specialize in the design, installation, supervision, and commissioning of complex infrastructure projects. Our commitment to quality and safety sets us apart in the industry.

Our Sustainability & Environmental Policy

🌍 Our Environmental Policy

At Marshk, we are committed to conserving natural resources through efficient energy use and reduction of emissions. We believe that sustainable infrastructure development is not just an obligation but a responsibility to future generations.

Our environmental initiatives include:

  • ✓ Energy Efficiency: Implementation of renewable energy solutions and minimization of energy consumption in all operations
  • ✓ Emission Reduction: Continuous efforts to reduce carbon footprint and greenhouse gas emissions
  • ✓ Sustainable Materials: Selection of environmentally friendly materials and responsible sourcing practices
  • ✓ Waste Management: Proper disposal and recycling of construction waste and project materials
  • ✓ Community Impact: Ensuring our projects contribute positively to local environmental sustainability
